OBJECTIVE OF VISIT AND TOPIC

The main objective of the visit was to know the lifestyle of the village people and to find their problem. Based on the study of lifestyle and problem, planning of technical solutions, and preparing reports for further product development. This project is on DRAINAGE STYSTEM FOR VILLAGE. It was observed that the drainage system in the village was very poor. Villagers were facing problems due to it. Hence in this project analysis on how this problem can be solved or overcome is discussed.
