A tree-foot-wide,four-foot-deep pit exist in every home. Another smaller pit that is a foot deep first filters the water. The small pit is open with small grating on it, so that the water can easily sip in. Sedimentation happens in the 1st pit. And that water moves to the other pit that has gravel that filters the water more. This pit has a covered top. Now from this big pit, there is a little separation distance between the water table. The now clean water from the big pit eventually gets added in the water table. This dry pit needs a soil that can soak in water. This village that we went has the soil that soaks water so this method can be used.
