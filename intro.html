Introduction

Description of the topic

Drainage is a natural or artificial process of draining/removing water or other type of liquids from a place/area. In easy terms it is a transportation system for wastewater, flood or rainwater, human waste etc. There are various methods of drainage. According to construction: natural or artificial. According to function: open, closed, or surfaced. Lastly according to implementation: house drains, industrial drains agricultural drains and roadside drains.

General background/ Identified problem

We know that majority of the Indian population still live in the rural areas. But still a proper drainage system in these places is not available. Even if they are available, they are not as per the required standards. Recently in a trip to this village from the university, we saw that the drainage system in this area is also very poor. Improper drainage facility especially in the case of this village which is in a low-lying area leads to overflowing gutters, flooding, property loss, breeding of insects, soil

OBJECTIVE OF VISIT AND TOPIC

The main objective of the visit was to know the lifestyle of the village people and to find their problem. Based on the study of lifestyle and problem, planning of technical solutions, and preparing reports for further product development. This project is on DRAINAGE STYSTEM FOR VILLAGE. It was observed that the drainage system in the village was very poor. Villagers were facing problems due to it. Hence in this project analysis on how this problem can be solved or overcome is discussed.

SCOPE OF THE PROJECT

• People of the village will have access to proper drainage system
• It shall be a cost-effective system so that it is efficient of the villagers
• It will create better environment for plant growth
• To provide healthy environment
• The provision of proper drainage system will eliminate the problems cause by growing bacteria and mosquitoes
• Due to proper drainage the rate of illness will decrease, and the living standard of the people will improve
